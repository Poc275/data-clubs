Introducing Data Clubs

A Proprietary Operational Data Sharing Framework

Data as a service

Airlines are undergoing a profound shift in their view of how data can transform their operational and maintenance performance. The imperative to achieve substantial year-on-year efficiency savings driving the search for a credible partner with subject matter and data expertise.

Why Data Clubs?

Airlines remain unable to reap the promise of big data. Digital is uniquely suited to re-imagine data as a collaborative effort and have established Data Clubs.

How we can help

Data Clubs are a fusion of world class engineering and thought leadership in the pragmatic use of Big Data, A.I. and Machine Learning. A highly secure, highly visual platform where relative performance can be understood in a secure-proof environment.

What we can do for you

Powerful Data Reports

Gain powerful reports providing insight into your data. Interactive by design, the value of insight is multiplied as aggregated data sets provide more ammunition for our world-class data scientists to provide incredible insight to airlines.

Leading Data Analytics

Through Data Clubs you get access to world-leading Data Scientists and our carefully selected Small & Medium Enterprise (SME) partners who will deliver analytics fueled by latest technology in Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Secure Data Ingestion

We ingest data in a secure manner. You have full control over your data and how it gets utilised in Data Clubs. We offer multiple sources of data ingestion to our customers and 3rd party data publishers. All supported by PODAF.
